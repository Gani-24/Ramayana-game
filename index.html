Welcome to Ramayana Snakes and Ladders

Rules of the Game:

  • Roll the dice to move your token on the board.
  • Collect special items along the way to gain advantages.
  • If you land on an Enemy, you may get demoted to a lower position.
  • But if you have weapons or Sanjeevani you collected in the game, then you are safe.
  • If you land on a Friend, you may get promoted to a higher position.
  • The first player to rescue Sita Devi wins.
